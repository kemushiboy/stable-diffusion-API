Privacy Policy
The author of this website (hereinafter referred to as "the author") has established the following privacy policy (hereinafter referred to as the "Policy") regarding the handling of user information on this website (hereinafter referred to as the "Site") .
Article 1 (Personal Information)
"Personal information" refers to "personal information" as defined in the Act on the Protection of Personal Information, and includes information about living individuals that can identify specific individuals by name, date of birth, address, telephone number, contact information, and other descriptions, as well as data related to appearance, fingerprints, voiceprints, and health insurance card insurer numbers.
Article 2 (Method of Information Collection)
In order to use this site, users are asked to enter personal information such as name and address, and attribute information such as gender and age.
Article 3 (Purpose of Information Collection and Use)
Collected personal information is used only for the image generation function on this site. Personal information will not be stored on the server and will be deleted.
The collected attribute information and generated images, which are not personal information, will be stored on the server and freely available to the author.
Article 4 (Provision of Information to Third Parties)
Personal information will not be provided to third parties on this site, as it will not be stored on the server and will be deleted.
Attribute information collected and generated images that are not personal information may be transferred or sold to third parties.
Article 5 (Disclosure of Personal Information)
The Site will not disclose any information about you, as personal information will not be stored on the server and will be deleted, and attribute information and generated images will be stored in a non-personally identifiable manner.
